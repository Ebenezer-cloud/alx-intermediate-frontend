Understanding Semantic HTML

Published on September 10, 2024

Introduction

Semantic HTML helps improve the accessibility and SEO of your website. In this post, we’ll explore its benefits and how to implement it.

Main Content

Using elements like <article>, <section>, and <header> gives meaning to your page.

These elements provide meaning to the structure of your HTML document, making it easier for browsers and assistive technologies to understand the content.

Conclusion

By adopting semantic HTML, you enhance your site's accessibility, improve SEO, and make the content easier to navigate.
